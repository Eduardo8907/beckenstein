When you entrust Beckenstein Fabrics to create custom upholstery and slipcovers, you can be confident you’ll receive the world-class service and attention to detail on which we’ve built our reputation. Quality is at the forefront of everything we do—it’s why our upholstery furniture shop in New York City has flourished for nearly a century. Count on us to skillfully recover your furniture with the fabric of your choice, tailoring the look to your style and comfort.

Custom Slipcovers Breathe New Life Into Furniture

If the upholstery on your sofa, loveseat, armchair, ottoman, sectional, or other furniture piece looks worn, Beckenstein custom slipcovers are the ideal choice. Slipcovers give your furniture a fresh look now while leaving you with the option of reupholstering later.

Choose from Thousands of Fabric Styles, Colors, and Textures

Simply select the fabric of your choice from our extensive catalog, and one of our craftsmen will come to your home to take measurements. Then we’ll get to work crafting custom covers that fit your furniture seamlessly. Don’t scour New York City for slipcovers—count on the experts at Beckenstein. Pick-up and delivery service is available for all upholstery: Call 646.395.3697 or email us at info@beckensteinfabrics.com.

Don’t hesitate to contact us for information about our new filling for cushions and custom furniture, and ask about our upholstery services.
